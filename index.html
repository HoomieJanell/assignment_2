About Me

In only 2.5 years, I received a Bachelor of Arts degree from Texas A&M University, College Station, where I majored in Communication, minored in Psychology, and graduated Magna Cum Laude with a 3.8 GPA. Following graduation, I moved to Austin, Texas, where I made my way into the tech industry and worked as a Content Quality Associate at the Favor Delivery Headquarters. There, I built out menus for the app and also sparked an interest in coding.

In June of 2019, I became a Business Development Representative at LogicMonitor, a SaaS-based and agentless IT monitoring solution that provides visibility into both on-prem and cloud environments from a single UI. Although doing outbound sales for a software company can be interesting, I wanted to pursue more technical roles. In order to do so, I enrolled in a coding bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin, where I learned full stack development, while at the same time balancing my full-time position as BDR. After receiving my Certificate in Full-Stack Development, I attained a position as Junior Web Developer, where I'm currently able to continue to grow and expand my knowledge and skill set.
